Society of Women Journalists (SWJ)

Project Overview

The Society of Women Journalists site, by Dr. Laura Vorachek, aims to. . .

- Show the identities of women working as journalists at the turn of the 20th century

- Map networks of social and professional support among female journalists

- Recover a detailed history of the early years of the SWJ

- Centralize SWJ membership lists and make them more widely accessible
